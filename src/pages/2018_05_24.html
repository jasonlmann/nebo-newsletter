Memorial Day weekend is here, and Nebo Lodge is opening for the season!

Our opening menu has many of your favorites and a few surprises: fresh Turner Farm spring greens, North Haven lamb and pork, fresh-caught halibut, and house-made ramen with island pork belly in a savory chicken broth.

Happy Hour!

We're celebrating the arrival (finally!) of warm weather with happy hours at Nebo from 5–7pm every Friday May 25–June 22.

  • $5 for all beer in a can (and we have some good ones!)
  • $6 Punch Bowl
  • $7 House wines
  • And, for $10 each try our Lamb Sliders, Carnitas Tacos, General Tso’s Chicken, Pork Belly Steamed Buns, and Vegetable Tempura

North Haven has more than just the inn! We have miles of great roads for biking and beautiful beaches for an impromptu picnic. We’ve got a nine-hole island golf course with water views. Take a tour of the Turner Farm greenhouses or enjoy a late afternoon pint at North Haven Brewing Co.

Rooms are still available for Memorial Day weekend, as well as some weekends in June.
