[firstname,fallback=Friend], it’s spring on North Haven! Lilacs are threatening to bloom, the ballfield is a brilliant green, and Calderwood Hall is open again!

They’re famous for their pizza, but we’re probably most excited for the return of the Saturday morning cinnamon rolls! (Don’t get between us and the last one.)

And while you’re at the Hall, don’t forget to stop in at North Haven Brewing Company. They have new varieties and old favorites already on tap. It’s a perfect place to relax with a pint on a late spring afternoon.

Even in our little island town, there’s too much to do in just one night—so make a weekend of it! With a few rooms still available for Memorial Day weekend and in June, you can come experience it for yourself before the summer rush gets here.
