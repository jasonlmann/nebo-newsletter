Spring on the farm

You’ve heard of farm-to-table… but why not choose the table at the farm? This summer, Nebo Lodge continues its summer tradition of barn suppers—meals served family-style in the gorgeous barn at the island’s historic Turner Farm. These are special events, great for special occasions or just to celebrate the harvest from the soil and sea.

Spring on the farm means new life: from sprouting greens to new calves. Come to the Turner Farm’s Open Farm Day on Saturday, May 21st from 9:30am to 12:30pm and meet the newest resident of the farm—our Jersey calf Pennyroyal—or our 30 baby pigs!

Our amazing farmers will be there to answer your questions and show you around the historic farm—which is once again producing fresh produce, meat, and artisan cheeses for the island and elsewhere.

Nebo visitors love to visit the farm, and you are welcome to visit daily, attend a barn dinner, or plan your special event at the farm. For more information and hours on the, check out the Turner Farm website!
